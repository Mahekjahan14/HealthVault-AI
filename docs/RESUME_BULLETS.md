# Resume Bullets

- Built HealthVault AI, an AI-powered patient history intelligence platform using React, Node.js, MongoDB, Groq, and Gemini Vision.
- Designed a lifelong medical history repository with allergies, diseases, surgeries, medications, reports, doctor visits, and health timeline.
- Implemented AI medical summary generation and doctor copilot Q&A to help doctors understand patient history faster.
- Developed medical report analyzer with OCR-based extraction and automatic update of patient records.
- Added AI healthcare triage using symptoms and historical medical context to suggest urgency level and specialist.
