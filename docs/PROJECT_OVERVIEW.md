# HealthVault AI Project Overview

## Problem
Patient medical records are scattered across hospitals, clinics, labs, and prescriptions. Doctors often do not get the complete patient history quickly, which can delay diagnosis and cause repeated tests.

## Solution
HealthVault AI creates a unified lifelong patient health profile. It stores medical history and uses AI to summarize, analyze reports, assist doctors, and perform triage.

## Main modules
1. Patient Registration and Profile
2. Lifelong Medical History Repository
3. Health Timeline Visualization
4. Medical Report Analyzer using OCR + AI
5. AI Health Summary Generator
6. Healthcare Triage Assistant
7. Doctor Copilot Q&A over patient history
8. Simple Role-Based Login Demo

## Unique value
The project is not a basic symptom checker. The main value is patient history intelligence: a doctor can understand years of patient history in seconds.

## Safety note
The app gives AI-assisted support only. It does not replace a doctor and does not provide final diagnosis.
